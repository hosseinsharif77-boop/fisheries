Our Story

A Yorkshire Tradition,
Reborn

We recently took over this beloved historic chippy to ensure the authentic taste of British fish and chips lives on in Heckmondwike. While our methods are traditional—fried in beef dripping, battered by hand—our vision is to bring this heritage to a new generation.

We've kept the classic recipes our elders love, while introducing new snack-size deals and online ordering. From a quick afternoon bite to a family dinner, every meal leaves the kitchen with the same care.

Our Promise

A Tradition Worth
Protecting

Market Fisheries has been serving the heart of Heckmondwike with what we believe is the finest fish and chips in the region — cooked the proper way, the traditional way, in beef dripping.

"Every piece of fish we fry carries 100 years of British chippy heritage — nothing frozen, nothing compromised."

We source our haddock fresh, batter it by hand, and fry it golden in real beef dripping the way it was always meant to be done. From a quick takeaway to sitting down with family, every meal leaves the kitchen with the same care.
